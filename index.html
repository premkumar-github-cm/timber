YOUR CHOICE

Your furniture will be exactly what you would like, and we will help with its completion.

Selection of material and manufacturer

Individual approach to the choice of materials. We will show samples and explain the performance properties.

Selection of fittings and selection of mechanisms

Many people do not guess, but the cost of accessories can sometimes be up to 30% of the budget of the product itself. We will help you choose the option that suits you in terms of price / quality ratio.

Choice of finishing

High gloss is more expensive than matte enamel and oil, and laminated materials are the cheapest. We select any colors, agree on color proofs before launching.

We use only premium fittings - we provide a choice of several quality suppliers such as: Blum, Grass, Hettich, Kessebhomer, Vibo, Hafele, Vauth-Sagel and others
